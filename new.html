ABOUT ACTOR VIJAY

Vijay was born as Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar on 22 June 1974 in Madras (now Chennai), Tamil Nadu.[14] His father, S. A. Chandrasekhar, is a Tamil film director[15] and his mother, Shoba Chandrasekhar, is a playback singer and carnatic vocalist.[16] His father is of Christian Vellalar descent and his mother is a Hindu.[17][18] Vijay was baptised at the age of 12.[17] Vijay had a sister, Vidhya, who died when she was just two years old.[19] Initially attending Fathima Matriculation Higher Secondary School[20] at Kodambakkam, Vijay later joined the Balalok Matriculation Higher Secondary School[21] at Virugambakkam and went on to pursue a degree in Visual Communications from Loyola College. While studying at the age of 18, he asked his father if "he would launch him".[22]


HIS CARRIER IN TAMIL INDUSTRY

During the same year, Lokesh Kanagaraj was selected as the director of Vijay's film Master produced by Xavier Britto.[111] Master was originally scheduled to be released on 9 April 2020, however, it was postponed due to the COVID-19 pandemic.[112] It was released on 13 January 2021 in theatres.[113] Master was the highest-grossing film in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) with a box office collection of $1.4 million within two days, surpassing the Hollywood releases Wonder Woman 1984 and Tenet within the same country.[114] It was the first Indian film to periodically hold the number one spot globally for its year. The film was screened at the International Film Festival of Alberta, Canada.[115] Upon release, it received mixed to positive reviews from viewers and critics.[116][117][118] Eastern Eye, a British magazine, called Master "an entertaining massy action film" but stated that "the runtime is a bit long".[119] The film collected ₹300 crore worldwide and was declared a box office hit.[120] The combined gross earnings of Mersal, Sarkar, Bigil and Master were determined to be over ₹1000 crore according to trade analysts.[121] The dark comedy action film Beast was released theatrically on 13 April 2022, worldwide.[122][123] Beast received mixed reviews from critics.[124][125] Roger Ebert, an American film critic website, reviewed the film Beast with 3/5 stars and called "Vijay as a stoic multifaced tiger with a multifaceted avatar".[126] Beast was the highest grossing Tamil film in US premiere shows.[127][128] Beast broke box office collection records in the international markets of New Zealand, Australia and Singapore.[129] The film was commercially successful, collecting ₹300 crore worldwide,[130][131][132][133] and was one of the most viewed films on Netflix globally in 2022.[134][135][136][137] It was the first Tamil film released theatrically in Uzbekistan.[138] The action drama film Varisu was produced by Telugu film producer Dil Raju and directed by Vamshi Paidipally. Thaman S composed the music and background score for the film.[139] Varisu was released theatrically worldwide on 11 January 2023 to polarizing reviews, while Vijay's meta-comedy acting was identified.[140][141][142][143][144] Varisu collected ₹300 crore at the box office.[145][146] In 2023, he starred in the action thriller, Leo, playing the titular role directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj, marking his second collaboration with him after Master. It released worldwide on 19 October 2023 in Tamil, Hindi, Telugu, and Kannada languages.[147] The film's soundtrack was composed by Anirudh, in his fourth collaboration with Vijay.[148][149] On 21 May 2023, director Venkat Prabhu and AGS Entertainment announced Vijay's next movie, tentatively titled Thalapathy 68.
